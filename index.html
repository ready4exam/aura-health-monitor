About AuraHealth Repository

What is AuraHealth?

AuraHealth is a quiet, clean, and local-first Personal Wellness & Vitals Companion. This repository is designed to serve as a private, meditative digital journal for logging physiological metrics, tracking daily habits, and visualizing your general health path over time.

Rather than dealing with complicated, cluttered clinical dashboards or trackers that sell your personal health logs, AuraHealth offers a peaceful aesthetic with absolute offline privacy.

Key Application Capabilities

This completely functional web workspace includes the following capabilities:

  • Daily Vitals Logging: Track Heart Rate (bpm), Blood Pressure (systolic/diastolic), Sleep hours, Water intake, Active exercise minutes, and general Mood.
  • One-Tap Quick Trackers: Add hydration (+250ml) or movement (+15m) to your active day instantly with simple, intuitive buttons.
  • Customizable Quick Trackers: Adjust the default values for one-tap water intake and active minute additions to suit your immediate needs.
  • Dynamic SVG Trends: Interactive health graphs that dynamically render Sleep, Water, Active time, and Heart Rate trends with tooltips.
  • Custom Goals Setting: Set and customize sleep, hydration, and activity goals to update target metrics dynamically.
  • Real-time Vitals Assessor: Immediate visual clinical range feedback on heart rate and blood pressure inputs as you type.
  • Historical Ledger: Review your chronological wellness history, filter by mood, search by date, or delete entries seamlessly.

Technical Highlights & Privacy

This repository is built with simplicity, ease of deployment, and absolute privacy in mind:

Vanilla JS (ES6) HTML5 Canvas/SVG CSS Glassmorphism Zero Server Dependencies No Trackers

100% Local Storage: All data is stored directly in your computer's browser using localStorage. No health data ever leaves your device, keeping your personal metrics completely private and secure. You can clear your local data anytime using the Reset Data button in the ledger.
